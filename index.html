Features

BridgeBond uniquely connects sponsor control with verified in-country care and a transparent health‑credit system.

Dual-Sided Platform

Sponsor dashboard in the UK + patient portal in Nigeria — both tailored to their needs.

Health Credits

Pre-purchase traceable credits for consults, tests, meds and home care — no more untraceable cash.

Verified Care & Pharmacies

We partner with licensed doctors, NAFDAC-approved pharmacies and accredited labs for safe care.

Domiciliary Care

UK-style home care visits with MAR records and escalation to clinicians when needed.

EHR & Clinical Governance

Persistent records, audit trails and clinical decision support for safer prescriptions.

Low-Bandwidth Access

WhatsApp and SMS pathways for patients with limited internet access.

Mission

To empower diaspora families with tools, transparency and trusted partners so they can actively fund, manage and monitor healthcare for loved ones — ensuring dignity, safety and continuity of care across borders.

Vision

In ten years, BridgeBond Health will be the global standard for diaspora‑sponsored care infrastructure — a trusted bridge that ensures no family is left behind because of distance.
