Our Vision

This interactive prototype is a core component of the pre-MVP stage for Bridge Bond Health. It demonstrates our commitment and the viability of our business model by simulating key user journeys and functionalities. This work represents a significant portion of our initial investment, far beyond a simple financial figure.

  • Interactive UX: Experience the flow from funding to service delivery.
  • Dynamic Dashboard: See how balances and service counts update in real-time.
  • Financial Transparency: The simulated activity log shows every transaction for a single source of truth.
