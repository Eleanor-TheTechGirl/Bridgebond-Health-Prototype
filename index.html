Everything in one place

A dual‑sided platform for diaspora sponsors and local patients — built for transparency, safety, and continuity of care.

1

Sponsor Dashboard

Book consults, approve prescriptions, fund services with Health Credits, and track visits & vitals.

2

Patient App

Simple, multilingual patient portal for appointments, meds, reminders, and education.

3

Teleconsultations

Secure video with verified clinicians. 3‑way calls so sponsors can attend if the patient wishes.

4

Home & Social Care

Vetted carers provide UK‑style domiciliary visits with MAR sheets and wellbeing checks.

5

EHR & CDS

Persistent records with Clinical Decision Support to flag risks, interactions, and missed refills.

6

Authentic Medications

NAFDAC‑approved pharmacy partners with refill tracking and adherence nudges.
